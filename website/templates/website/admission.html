ADMISSION DOCUMENT REQUIREMENTS AND PROCEDURES

  1. Accomplish Application Form and submit this to the GS Office together with the:
    • Transcript of Records
    • Certificate of Honorable Dismissal
    • Two (2) Letters of Recommendation
    • Two (2) 2x2 ID pictures
  2. Take RBGS Admission Test
  3. Set/ask for an interview schedule from Graduate School Office
  4. After the evaluation, accepted students should submit to the Registrar’s Office the Transcript of Records, Honorable Dismissal, and Enrollment Form.
